TEAM NAME

ME AND THE BOIZ

Having done the discussion thoroughly, we decided to name the team “Me and the boiz”. The first reason why we choose this name is that all members are boys. Furthermore, we think everyone in the team should be respected. Therefore, we put “Me” in the beginning to show respect and put “the boiz” as the mention of the other team members to show consolidation 

Nguyen Luu Quoc Bao
(me)

As can be seen from the figure, my personality leans towards introvert and thinking, which means that I can organize and structure plans and projects for the team. Besides, I can take over some technical aspects, such as making slides and cover pages. However, I should find teammates who have strength in communication and languages, which are needed for productive presentations. Fortunately, through five weeks of studying at RMIT, I have made lots of friends whose personalities are suitable to be my teammate. Without hesitation, I have invited four of them to be my teammate. They are Nguyen Trong Minh Long, Nguyen Dang Nhat, Nguyen Quang Duy, and Do Duc Anh.

Nguyen Trong Minh Long

Long is an active boy with good use of language, which will be helpful in connecting team members. He is also my first friend at RMIT. To compare his character to me, we have not only some same points but also some contrast points. For instance. In the share personailies, he is also an introvert who uses a lot of judging, which is the same as me. However, he own an emotional sensation. A clear evidence of this is that he have 67 percent of feelings, which is 28 percent in my result  

Nguyen Dang Nhat

Nhat shares mostly the same personalities to me such as introvert and judging. Nevertheless, his high index of feeling is very suitable to be my teammate since my result points out that I am lacking of feeling. Turning to Nhat’s abilities, this warm boy can make practical plans, which will be the navigation for the team in the future since we will have numerous tasks to be completed.

Nguyen Quang Duy

Duy is also an introvert. However, he is the same to me in the feeling aspect, even his index of feeling is lower than mine. In terms of Duy’s abilities, he can do well on individual tasks but not in team projects as he has a problem with communication. However, we will help him to break the ice and become more out-going to everyone.

Hoang Duc Anh

Last but not least, Duc Anh is the only extravert in my team. He has high index of emotion and feeling, which is contrast to most of member of my team. It is reasonable as a team must have a variety kind of people. Anh is the oldest member of my teams. We can use his experience in making projects and presentation to make our project as good as possible. 

SUMMARY

With these combination, I will be the organizer, who organize the tasks, combining with Nhat's plan to optimize the efficiency, we will work together with the communication and technical support of Long and Duy to produce a superb project, which is presented by the good word choice of Duc Anh. Hopefully, we will be successful in the near future.
