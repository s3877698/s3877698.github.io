IT work

            Mr. Duong Thanh Huy is the quality controller of Bigin company, which is an Australia company. His main work is to check whether the program such as websites, apps, or software is good enough for launching. He checks every case that can happen and act as a user to use the products. In case of bugging, he will mark it and write a report to the developer office to fix it immediately. Otherwise, he will contact the customer for running demo version before launching it widely. 

As a connector between IT users and developers. He mainly interacts with customers who will make the requirement of the product. Besides, he works with numerous IT professional including his managers and company leader. Basically, he meets those IT professionals to discuss problems popping up during the progress, which he does not have right to decide. However, he hardly meets investor as it is not his field, which is usually done by the team leaders.

As a quality controller, he mostly spent his time at his office in the company testing and manage the projects to meet the released day. His frequently working day is from 8 am to 5 pm, with a lunch break of 2 hours from midday to 2 pm. Therefore, he finds it no stressful emotion as he has enough time for resting.

The most challenging aspect that he faces is the progress management of the developer office. Since he is the connector between customers and developers, he will be responsible for all lateness of developers with the customers, which causes him a lot of annoying. Therefore, he has to remind and fasten the progress and tasks of the developer as fast as possible to launch the product punctually.
