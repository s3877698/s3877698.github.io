AI and machine learning

What is the state of art of those technoligies?

AI (artificial intelligence) is intelligence demonstrated by machines. This technology often revolves around the use of algorithms, which are coded by humans. In terms of machine learning, this technology is a branch in collecting algorithms that the AI learned. Machine learning helps to input data into the computer, which helps it learn algorithms fast. Machine learning focuses on the development of computer programs. AI and machine learning are almost combined with each other in every case. The state of art of those new technologies is very useful for humans to complete the job and others' basic activities in life, such as driving cars. It automates every step of the algorithms to run everything as smoothly as possible since it is coded and being taught day by day by the scientist about how to handle problems.  In detail, it can be coded to think and act like a human. For instance, the self-driving car applied by the AI will automatically avoid an object on the road by turning left or right, which is similar to the thought of a driver. By collecting a tuple of driving habits and skills, artificial intelligence is gradually grown up with data, which makes it more intelligent in handle the situation on the road. This intelligence may surpass the human ability in analyzing and reflecting as a situation can be analyzed and handled in a millisecond.
